5 FAMOUS INDIAN ACTORS

Five icons of Indian cinema—Shah Rukh Khan, Rajinikanth, Mohanlal, Vijay, and Allu Arjun—have not only dominated their respective regional industries but have also set massive box office benchmarks,
collectively showcasing the incredible scale and diversity of the Indian film industry.

Shah Rukh Khan, the "King of Bollywood," currently holds the top spot among them with his 2023 action thriller Jawan, which grossed over 1,148 crore worldwide, closely followed by his other 2023 blockbuster, Pathaan.

Rajinikanth, the "Superstar," saw his biggest success with the 2018 science-fiction epic 2.0, earning over 699 crore globally, and recently starred in the 605 crore-grossing Jailer, which also featured a notable cameo by Mohanlal.

Mohanlal, "The Complete Actor," stands as a foundational pillar in Malayalam cinema, and his film L2: Empuraan is reported to be his highest-grossing film, collecting approximately ₹268 crore worldwide.
Prior to this, his 2016 action blockbuster Pulimurugan was the first Malayalam film to officially cross the ₹100 crore mark, a massive milestone for the industry.

Vijay, the "Thalapathy," his most successful film to date is the 2023 action thriller Leo, which crossed the 595 crore mark worldwide.

Finally, Allu Arjun, the "Stylish Star," has achieved significant pan-India success with the 2021 film Pushpa: The Rise, which earned approximately 360 crore, and whose sequel, Pushpa 2: The Rule, has recently become one of the highest-grossing Indian films of all time.

This collection of highest-grossing films perfectly illustrates the massive appeal and financial power wielded by these five cinematic legends.
